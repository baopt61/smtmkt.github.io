EVN SPC Culture

5.1.Core Value

Vision

Vietnam Electricity (EVN) is a leading economic group in the power sector in Vietnam and in the region, plays a key role in ensuring the national energy security.

Mission

To sufficiently meet the electric energy demand of customers with better and better quality and services.

Slogan

Enlightening trust

Core value

Quality – Trust

EVN honors this value with the goal of building trust and credibility by product and service quality, honesty and responsibility. EVN commits to every partner that assurance of quality will be a top priority and the increasing demands of customers will be satisfactorily met.

Devotion - Wisdom

EVN desires its employees to be devoted and have a high sense of responsibility. All EVN’s employees dedicate their full energy and brainpower to fulfill their works effectively, wholeheartedly serve to satisfy customers and for the happiness of the people.

Cooperation - Sharing

EVN respects cooperation and harmony, and always cooperates in the spirit of honesty, fairness, sharing, working closely together to solve all the problems , growing together, acting together for the success, and progress of EVN, of each member of the organization and of EVN’s partners.

Creativity- Efficiency

Creativity is a development leverage of EVN. EVN desires to create a favorable environment for people to enhance creativity, technological innovation, management processes to deliver the highest performance, putting EVN to the leading position in the power sector in the country and in the region.

5.2. Ethical standards

Ethical standards

Every action of EVN is human oriented , for the sake of human being.

EVN acts for the joy and the happiness of the people. EVN builds relationships with customers, partners, colleagues, investors, the society by goodwill, friendship, and human spirit. In the operations of EVN, people is always centered, internally within EVN, employees are considered the most precious assets to help EVN succeed.

EVN always honors sense of responsibility and devotion dedicated to the work and customers

The fundamental guiding principle of EVN is to fulfil its obligations in the capacity of an important economic group of the economy and of the country. Every member of EVN commits itself to act for the national interest and the prosperity of the Fatherland.

EVN commits itself to respect the laws, to act unbiasedly, honestly, impartially and transparently

In all of its activities, EVN always complies with all provisions of the current laws, internal management regulations of EVN and EVN’s subsidiaries. EVN respects rules, etiquette in communication, courtesy, honesty in relationships, objectivity, transparency, fairness and equality in relationships with external stakeholders (customers, partners, communities) as well as internal stakeholders (employees, owners, managers).

Strength in every action of EVN is consensus and collective intelligence

EVN has a spirit of solidarity, partnership and innovative working which is a synergy to achieve the set targets.

EVN strives to deserve the trust of the country and the people

EVN respects its commitments to customers, partners, being faithful to spirits of the core values, fulfilling commitments by its practical actions.

5.3. Enforcing Culture

Commitment to the Party and the Government, "Electricity will be one step ahead"

EVN commits itself to fulfill the tasks entrusted by the Party and the Government, to ensure a stable, safe, reliable power supply to the development of the country. EVN can function as a reliable tool for the Party and the Government to stabilize the macro-economic policy, contribute to the prosperity of country, to enable the country to keep pace with other advanced countries in the region.

Each employee of EVN is fully aware of the core role and mission placed on EVN for the national energy security.

Commitment to its employees "Human resources are the most valuable asset"

- Always make the labor’s employment and life secured.

- Apply fair and transparent treatment policies.

- Encourage and appreciate initiatives and best facilitate realization of the initiatives.
